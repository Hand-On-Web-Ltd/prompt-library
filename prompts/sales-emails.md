# Sales Email Prompts

## 1. Cold Outreach Email

```
Write a cold email to [job title] at a [type of company].

I want to offer them [your service/product].
The main benefit for them is [key benefit].

Rules:
- Subject line under 6 words
- Email under 100 words
- One clear ask at the end
- No "I hope this email finds you well"
- Sound like a real person, not a sales bot
```

## 2. Follow-Up Email (No Reply)

```
I sent a cold email to [person/company] about [what you offered] and got no reply. It's been [X days].

Write a follow-up that:
- Doesn't guilt trip them
- Adds new value (a tip, stat, or insight)
- Has a softer ask than the first email
- Stays under 80 words
```

## 3. Proposal Email

```
Write an email to send with a proposal/quote for [service].

Client: [name]
Project: [brief description]
Price: £[amount]
Timeline: [how long]

The email should:
- Recap what we discussed
- Highlight the main deliverables
- Create gentle urgency without being pushy
- Ask them to reply or book a call
```

## 4. Re-engagement Email

```
We haven't heard from [client name] in [X months]. They used to [buy/use our service].

Write a check-in email that:
- Feels natural, not salesy
- Mentions something relevant (new feature, seasonal offer, industry change)
- Makes it easy for them to reply
- Doesn't mention that they've been quiet
```

## 5. Referral Ask

```
Write an email asking a happy client for referrals.

Client: [name]
What we did for them: [service/result]

The email should:
- Thank them genuinely
- Ask if they know anyone who might need similar help
- Make it easy (suggest they just forward the email or intro us)
- Not offer a bribe — keep it classy
- Under 100 words
```

## 6. Meeting Request

```
Write an email requesting a meeting with [person/role] at [company].

Purpose: [what you want to discuss]
Suggested format: [call/video/in-person]
Duration: [15 min/30 min]

Give them 2-3 time options and make it easy to say yes.
Keep it short — under 80 words.
```

## 7. Post-Meeting Follow-Up

```
I just had a meeting with [person] about [topic]. Here's what we discussed:
- [point 1]
- [point 2]
- [point 3]

Write a follow-up email that:
- Thanks them for their time
- Summarises the key points
- Lists clear next steps with who's doing what
- Sets a deadline or follow-up date
```
